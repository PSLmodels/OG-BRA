# Calibration of Government Parameters

## Government Spending as a Share of GDP

We've calibrated the vector $\alpha_G=[0.118]$ from IMF Government Finance Statistics and $\alpha_T=[0.038]$ from the same source.

### Government spending on infrastructure as a share of GDP

We've calibrated the vector $\alpha_I=[0.0]$ in the default calibration.  This should be updated to reflect the actual share of government spending on infrastructure in Brazil.
